Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our service

How accurate are the transcripts?

+

Our transcription technology achieves over 95% accuracy for videos with clear audio. Factors like background noise, accents, and technical terminology may affect accuracy. Our Pro and Business plans include human review options for maximum accuracy.

Which languages do you support?

+

Our Basic plan supports English transcription. Pro plan adds support for Spanish, French, German, Italian, and Portuguese. Business plan supports over 30 languages including Japanese, Chinese, Korean, Arabic, and many European languages.

How long does it take to generate a transcript?

+

Most transcripts are ready within minutes. A 10-minute video typically takes less than 1 minute to process. Longer videos on the Pro and Business plans may take 5-10 minutes. Processing time depends on video length and server load.

Can I edit the transcript after it's generated?

+

Yes, Pro and Business plans include an online editor where you can refine the transcript, adjust timestamps, and correct any errors. Basic plan allows copying the text to edit in your own application.

Is my data secure?

+

Yes, all video processing happens on secure servers. We don't store video content after processing is complete, and transcripts are only accessible to you. Business plans include additional security features like encrypted storage and custom retention policies.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

+

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time through your account settings. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the service.
